About Sangrur

Sangrur, a city of immense importance in the context of the glorius history and the heritage is located in the south-east province of the Punjab. The area of the city is spread over 3625 sq Km and has population more than 1,655,169.

History

The city of Sangrur is established and named after a Jatt called Sanghu. The district of Sangrur comprises of former administrative units, the states of Nabha, Jind, Patiala, Malerkotla and Ludhiana.

Tourism in Sangrur

The city is rich in culture and has a lot of attractive sites.

  • Gurudwara Nankian Sahib
  • The Four Gates: Patiala, Sunami, Dhuri and Nabha gate
  • Banasar Park
